Who we are

A growing 3D printing studio for Kenyan makers.

We help businesses, students and creators turn concepts into physical objects — from a single custom part to a small production run. Everything is printed, finished and quality-checked in our Nairobi workshop, so you get precise results without the wait or cost of shipping overseas.
